How we added Verizon
into the world
of Minecraft.

Believe it or not, until recently it was impossible to order a pizza from Minecraft. In fact, you couldn’t make a phone call, send a text message, or browse the web from the game even if you built a phone. Verizon engineers set out to solve these problems by doing what they do best -- creating a network where previously there was none. They’ve expanded the network to the virtual world of Minecraft by partnering with some of today’s biggest Minecraft experts, SethBling and Blockworks.
